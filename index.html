Boxes? and Lines?

Yes, boxes. And lines. Whenever we sit down to plan a new project, one of the first things we do is to sketch out the different layers of the system, how they fit together and what happens within each layer. Invariably we end up standing around a whiteboard or a sheet of blank paper, drawing boxes and lines.

Boxes to represent web servers, web sites, email systems, mobile devices, buttons on a web page, customers. Lines represent the flow of information, links between web pages, actions that people take, your organisation goals. Boxes and Lines help to understand how all these things fit together, so that we can put together a solution that meets your needs with minimal fuss.
